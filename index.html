Kongregate Games

These flash games were released and can surprisingly still be played on the web at kongregate.com/accounts/DaFlamingPotato. They use the same flash player emulator (ruffle) that I am using to display the games here on this page. For a while none of the games worked, but since ruffle was released they are playable again! If you remember any favorite flash games from your childhood, chances are you can play them again thanks to ruffle.
